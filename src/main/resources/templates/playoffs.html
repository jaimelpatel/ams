Playoffs

Here at NLG we want to reward teams competing hard on our site from both xp and cash ladders. Our Call of Duty: World War II leaderboards for Xbox One and Playstation 4 display the top teams in their respective ladders. These teams will be able to enter NLG hosted tournaments at the end of the season for free in a chance to win large cash prizes (details below). Just like XP teams, cash teams will earn and lose xp when they play any match whether it's a wager or part of a tournament.

XP and Cash team are both ranked on the leaderboards by their total xp gained before the season end. Call of Duty: World War II XP and Cash teams that are in the same platform and team size (Ex. Playstation 4 - Doubles) will play in the same tournament and compete for a huge cash prize. So if you're an XP player you can gain access to the tournament by topping the XP team leaderboards and if you're a cash player you can do it through the cash ladder. You can view the CoD: World War II leaderboards for Xbox One here and Playstation 4 here.


Playoffs Tournament Details

As of right now the top 64 teams (this number may change in the future) from each ladder will be eligible for playoff tournaments at the end of the season. As stated above XP and cash teams in the same ladder (Ex. Call of Duty: World War II - Playstation 4 - North America - Doubles) will play in the same tournament making the tournament bracket a maximum of 128. Singles will have less teams so the top 32 from the xp and cash ladders will make playoffs for a maximum of 64 teams per tournament. The first NLG season runs from January 1st, 2018 to April 7th, 2018. Anything before or after this time period is considered pre-season. The season 1 playoff tournaments will be held on April 14th, 2018 for all Xbox One ladders and April 15th, 2018 for all Playstation 4 ladders.

Tournament Prizes (for each console):

• Team (4v4) - $1600.00 ($1200.00 for winning team and $400.00 for runner up)
• Doubles (2v2) - $800.00 ($600.00 for winning team and $200.00 for runner up)
• Singles (1v1) - $400.00 ($300.00 for winning team and $100.00 for runner up)

These leaderboards are for North American teams as are NLG Playoffs for now. We will monitor our European support and if there are enough EU teams in our ladders then we will add leaderboards and playoffs for them. EU teams can still play free xp matches or wager matches against other EU teams in their ladder and enter any NLG tournaments created for EU players/teams.
