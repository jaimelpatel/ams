1. NLGO reserves the right to change or modify the rules at any time. Users must be aware of rule changes before creating/accepting a match. Judgements and decisions can be made outside the current set of rules in order to preserve the integrity of fair play. By playing on NLGO you are signifying that you agree with the rules and regulations as well as the privacy policy and terms of service of this site.

2. All accounts and teams registered with NLGO are property of Universal Gaming LLC; purchasing, selling, or trading accounts and/or teams is not allowed. Perpetrators will have their accounts shut down and removed. NLGO reserves the right to terminate, suspend, or reset any accounts/teams that violate our terms and conditions.

3. Third party communication services are allowed. However, at least one member of each team must be able to communicate with NLGO staff in game if necessary.

4. Players must be 18 years or older to compete in cash matches or cash tournaments.

5. Players competing in any NLG tournament, must be a resident of the United States, Mexico, Puerto Rico and Canada. Players must not be a resident of the state of Arizona, Connecticut, Maryland or the territory of Quebec.
