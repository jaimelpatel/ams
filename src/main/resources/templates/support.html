Who are we?

We are a group of fellow gamers just like you! We got tired of dealing with the small payouts and questionable customer service at other competitive gaming websites so we decided to create our own. Here at Next Level Gaming we offer free and cash matches/tournaments with the highest payouts and strive to present the best customer service possible. Thank you for visiting our site and we hope you stay.

What games do you offer for play?

As of right now we offer strictly Call of Duty based titles for matches and tournaments on both the Playstation 4 and Xbox One. We will primarily be focused on the upcoming Call of Duty: World War II, however, we will include options to play some of the other recent Call of Duty titles including Call of Duty: Modern Warfare Remastered and Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare. We hope to add other games in the future!

Why don't you have credits?

Unlike other competitive gaming websites, we offer NLG Cash as our only currency to play both cash matches and cash tournaments. Our ultimate goal is to provide the best pressure free environment for our users to have fun while making some extra cash. We wanted to make the process of playing matches/tournaments as easy as we could for our users and forcing users to buy non-refundable credits for tournaments just so we could make a quick buck didn't make sense. Therefore we only make money when our users actually play wagers/tournaments because NLG Cash can be withdrawn after purchase at any time. NLG Cash has a 1 to 1 ratio with the US Dollar ($1.00 NLG Cash = $1.00 USD).

What is the difference between an XP and Cash team?

The main and most important difference is XP teams are for free to play matches meaning users only gain/lose XP on these teams. A cash team is used for both cash matches (wagers) and cash tournaments, but these teams also gain/lose xp on all matches they play whether it is a wager or tournament match. On NLG cash teams are used for both wagers and tournaments since our only form of currency is NLG Cash. This was done in order to smooth and simplify the process as much as possible for our users. Another difference is that for free to play XP teams we only allow each user to be on 2 teams for the same ladder (Example of a ladder: Call of Duty: World War II - North America - Playstation 4 - Doubles). A user cannot be a part of more than 2 teams with the same attributes as shown in the example. For cash teams however, we allow users to be on up to 3 teams in any given ladder.

How do NLG Playoffs work?

On NLG we have leaderboards for both cash and xp teams. As stated in the answer to the question directly above, both xp and cash teams gain xp when they play free matches or cash matches in a wager or tournament. The amount of xp these teams acquire determine their ranks on the leaderboards, for both xp and cash teams. At the end of the season the top teams from each ladder will be placed into a free to play tournament to win a giant cash prize. As of right now, we are only offering playoff tournaments for North American Call of Duty: World War II teams on both Playstation 4 and Xbox One. Start competing in matches and tournaments today to top the leaderboards! View World War 2 Xbox One leaderboards here and Playstation 4 leaderboards here. You can read and learn more about playoffs here.

How do I deposit/withdraw/transfer NLG cash?

After you have registered and are logged your NLG cash amount is displayed in a box in the top navbar on the right. If you click on that box, a dropdown will show a few options including "Deposit Cash", "Withdraw Cash" and "Transfer Cash". Select any of the options for your situation. Cash can also be bought/deposited by visiting the Shop. We only accept PayPal as a valid payment method for now which includes a 2.9% + $0.30 USD fee on every transaction. In order to withdraw, you must have your paypal email entered into your account information so we know where to send your money. You will send and receive payments to/from our business entity, Universal Gaming, LLC.

How do I receive my winnings for a wager match/tournament?

If you win a wager match/tournament the NLG cash will be instantly deposited into your NLG account. At that point you can withdraw the cash if you want it and we will send you the money via PayPal as fast as we can.
