About Us

Here at NLG we offer Call of Duty gamers competitive free and cash matches/tournaments. Our only form of currency is NLG cash which can be used to play both wagers and tournaments. NLG Cash can be withdrawn by our users at any time after purchase. Our payouts are the highest across all websites while also getting deposited instantly into our match/tournament winners' account. As of right now we only offer Call of Duty titles to compete in with our primary focus being on the newly released Call of Duty World War II. We strive to offer the best customer service we can because we want to show appreciation to anybody using NLG as their competitive gaming arena. As a group of gamers ourselves we wanted to create a environment and community where competition thrives. We welcome suggestions on any new games or features that our users would like to see in the future so feel free to let us know through a support ticket or social media. Thank you for visiting our website and please read more below about NLG playoffs and some features that we will look to be releasing in the future!
