Future Features

At NLG we are always striving to give our users the best competitive gaming experience. We released NLG with all the features we believe were necessary for our users, but we have a lot of other ideas that we think will make the experience even better. We will be developing these new features in the background and hope we can continue our work through NLG's success! Below are just some of the features we plan to work on and release in the future:

1. Android/iOS Mobile Application - A free to download mobile application where users can login to their NLG accounts and view their information. Users will be able to upload images taken from their phones as dispute proof for matches and receive live notifications about their upcoming matches and tournaments.


2. XP Tournaments - Tournaments for XP teams where the buy in is a certain amount of xp. Works the same way as a cash tournament except users are wagering their xp in a chance to win a large amount of xp if they come out on top. A teams xp can be wagered as the buyin so if that team is victorious the team will be given the sum of the xp pot.

3. Community Match Dispute Resolution - A system for our users to solve disputes for XP matches. After a certain amount of correctly resolved disputes by a user, they will receive xp points as an incentive. In order for a user to become eligible as a match reviewer, they must have a minimum of 20 matches played and maintain a low percentage of dispute claim losses. However, if a user is incorrectly deciding matches repetitively they will lose this privilege.
